Our Products

At GeoSky, we deliver cutting-edge geospatial solutions tailored to your needs. Our products are designed to provide precision, efficiency, and actionable insights for a wide range of industries. Explore our offerings:

Digital Terrain Models (DTM)

Our high-resolution Digital Terrain Models (DTM) provide accurate representations of the bare earth surface, excluding buildings, vegetation, and other above-ground features. Ideal for topographic mapping, flood modeling, and infrastructure planning.

Digital Surface Models (DSM)

Digital Surface Models (DSM) capture the earth's surface, including all natural and man-made features. Perfect for urban planning, 3D visualization, and environmental monitoring.

Tiled Models

Our tiled models offer seamless, high-detail 3D representations of large areas. Optimized for performance, they are ideal for GIS applications, virtual simulations, and large-scale project planning.

Orthophotos

Georeferenced and distortion-free, our orthophotos provide a true-to-scale aerial view of the terrain. Essential for mapping, land use analysis, and cadastral surveys.

Point Cloud

Our classified point clouds deliver precise 3D data for complex environments. Used in construction, forestry, and heritage preservation, they provide unmatched accuracy for detailed analysis.
